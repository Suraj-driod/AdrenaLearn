FAQs

Why wouldn't I just hire a full-time designer?
Great question! The annual salary of a full-time, senior-level designer now exceeds $100,000, plus benefits (and good luck finding one available). With our service, you get top-notch design expertise without the commitment.
How much work is delivered on every update?
On every update, you'll get about what a senior employee could realistically deliver in 2 days. Most updates are fairly chunky paired with a Loom video walkthrough explaining our thinking.
Are there any meetings?
Meetings and calls have their place, but we've found them to be less useful in this era of great async tools. Conducting our communication through Trello and Loom has proven to be much better.
